Description: Learn how to prepare a grilled cheese sandwich.


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on the outside of two slices of bread.
  2. Place the slices of bread in pan, buttered side down.
  3. Put a slice of cheese on the face-up side of each slice of bread. Cook on medium heat for 30 seconds, and then close the sandwich and cook each side for 60 seconds.
